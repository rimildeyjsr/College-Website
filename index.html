About Us

RNS Institute Of Technology was established in the year 2001 by Mr. R.N.Shetty, with Prof.M R Holla as the Principal,and a mission to impart high quality education in the field of engineering and technology.The Institute is run by the R.N Shetty Trust and the college is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgavi. Today the college is headed by Dr. M.K Venkatesha as the principal and Dr. H.N Shivashankar as the Director.
